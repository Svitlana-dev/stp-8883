Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is Legends Reborn free to play?

    Yes! It’s 100% free to play with optional in-app purchases for cosmetics and faster progress.

  • Can I play offline?

    Absolutely. Your heroes keep grinding and earning rewards while you’re away.

  • How many heroes can I collect?

    Over 50 unique mythic heroes at launch, with more added every season.

  • What platforms is the game available on?

    You can download it from Google Play and App Store. Android & iOS supported.

  • Is there PvP or just PvE?

    Both! You can battle in PvE campaigns or climb the arena leaderboard in PvP.

  • Do heroes have unique voices?

    Yes! Each main hero is voiced by professional actors from anime & game industries.

  • Can I play without spending money?

    Yes. The game is designed to be fully enjoyable without spending. Progress is fair.

  • Is there a guild system?

    Yes! You can join or create a guild, take part in alliance battles, and share loot.

  • What is the energy system like?

    Energy recharges over time or with rewards. You won’t be stuck for long.

  • Are there time-limited events?

    Weekly events with exclusive rewards and seasonal challenges keep things fresh.
