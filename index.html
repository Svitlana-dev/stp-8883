About this Game

Start your zero to hero adventure! Fight your way through a magnificent and massive world map by summoning and leveling up your mythic heroes, gathering with allies to hunt the legendary BOSS, occupying towers, and becoming the strongest one!

Legends Reborn combines easy and fun idle RPG mechanics with strategic exploration, incredible 3D visuals, and immersive voiceovers by the world’s best talents.

How to Play

  • Step 1

    Summon your first heroes and enter the battle

  • Step 2

    Equip and level up your team with loot and gear

  • Step 3

    Challenge powerful bosses and unlock new regions

  • Step 4

    Join a guild to access alliance features and tower wars

  • Step 5

    Upgrade your base, complete quests, and collect legendary rewards
